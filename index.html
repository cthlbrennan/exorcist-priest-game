Exorcist Priest

Finally home. Good to see the cat's doing well anyway.

After years of studying at seminaries across Europe, I'm finally on my way back home to Kilross.

How to Play

1. Type the prayers before the timer runs out in order to exorcise the demons.

2. Every character of every prayer must be typed accurately.

3. You have five health points - if you fail to type the prayer in the allotted time, you'll lose a health point.

4. If you fail, you'll be able to try again.

5. If you lose all five health points, you'll die - bad craic.

6. If you pass a round, you'll recover two additional health points before progressing to the next demon.
